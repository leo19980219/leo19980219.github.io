Pitch Perfect

This is midterm project of Signal and System.

Introduction

For a professional singer, singing on the right pitch is the basic and the most important thing to go. According to the study, however, 99% of people cannot reach the 100% accurate pitch even if they are professional singers. Thus, I intend to develope this app to help people singing on the right pitch.

The app is separated into two levels, each level has several tasks. First, the user can play the exammple which provide by the program. Then, finish the task by recording your voice to the app. Lately, you can get a score evaluated by the algorithm.
